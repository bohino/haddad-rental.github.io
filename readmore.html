Online Rent A Management System Provide


The Online Rental a Car Management System (ORCMS) contain the data on the vehicle fleet 
and information about the specific vehicles.

Online Rent a Car Management System contain all the information about cars and customer, 
customer easily hire any car to contact with owner of the car.

All the information about the car rents, specific car avail or not, pick up location,
pick up date and drop out date are store also in ORCMS. The scope of the ORCMS is to provide 
a clear and easy options to get information about the car and hire any car. 

It will help the store managers and corporate office when they need to file reports and check on
rentals etc.
